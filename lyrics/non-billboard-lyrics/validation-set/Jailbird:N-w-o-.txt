N-w-o-:Jailbird
A bird is there offering you a chance
to find salvation again
outside your dreams.
The animal spirit lives forever
in your mind and in your heart.
Answer the call!
Capture the spirit of the crow,
messenger of Heaven.
Be awake to the traveller of the sky.
Dream of the spirit world
when death is bringing salvation
and understanding.
Then,
you'll realize that Heaven
has opened its doors ...
... only for you.
